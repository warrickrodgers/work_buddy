# Core Reference Material on Leadership Frameworks

This document contains key frameworks and models for leadership and organizational performance.

## Transformational Leadership Theory
- Inspirational motivation through vision and purpose
- Individualized consideration for team development
- Intellectual stimulation for innovation
- Idealized influence through role modeling

## Situational Leadership Model
- Directing: High direction, low support
- Coaching: High direction, high support
- Supporting: Low direction, high support
- Delegating: Low direction, low support

## Emotional Intelligence Framework
- Self-awareness: Understanding personal emotions
- Self-regulation: Managing emotional responses
- Motivation: Driving towards achievement
- Empathy: Understanding others' perspectives
- Social skills: Building effective relationships

## Key Leadership Principles
1. Lead by example and maintain integrity
2. Communicate vision clearly and consistently
3. Empower team members with autonomy
4. Foster continuous learning and development
5. Adapt leadership style to situation and individuals

## Performance Management Models
- OKRs (Objectives and Key Results)
- SMART goals framework
- Balanced Scorecard method
- Performance contracts and accountability

## Organizational Culture Models
- Competing Values Framework (Quinn)
- Organizational Culture Profile
- Denison Organizational Culture Survey
